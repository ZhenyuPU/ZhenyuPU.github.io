# ABOUT ME

I mainly focus on the study of reinforcement learning (RL) in the complex physical system for optimization and control, especially in the application of the multi-energy system, energy management and workload scheduling of AI data centers. I am driven by the obsession of the real-world decision-making problems and the self learning of RL (magic of RL). I have researched the representation learning combined with RL to achieve the capture of the complex and coupled state information and dynamic prediction of latent space under uncertainty. Additionally, I have also explored end-to-end prediction-to-optimize framework that combines the training of prediction models with downstream optimization tasks to solve the decision-making under uncertainty.
